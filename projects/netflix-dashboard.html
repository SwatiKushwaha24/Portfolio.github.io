Netflix Dashboard

Explore The Dashboard

Project Overview

This Tableau dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Netflix's content library, derived from a dataset containing 6,234 entries. The dashboard highlights key trends in movies and TV shows, including their distribution by country, genre popularity, ratings, and release years. Below is a detailed breakdown of the insights uncovered.

Key Insights

Content Distribution

  • TV Shows account for 31.58% of the total content (1,969 entries)
  • The remaining 68.42% are Movies
  • Example content analyzed: The movie Shame (2014) with its rating and description

Genre Popularity

  • Documentaries and Stand-Up Comedy dominate the platform
  • Other popular genres include:
    • Dramas (International and Independent)
    • Comedies (often combined with Dramas or International Movies)
    • Children & Family Movies

Release Year Trends

  • Content spans multiple years with examples from 2014 to 2017 highlighted
  • Visualization of content production trends over time

Challenges & Solutions

Rating system standardization Normalized rating categories for comparison
